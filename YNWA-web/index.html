YOU'LL NEVER WALK ALONE

Sing the European ‘sporting anthem’ You’ll Never Walk Alone with athletes, sports fans, students and community allies in support of female-identified teams and civil liberties (gender equity?). Your footage will be layered into a democratic chorus and evolving collaborative video as inspiration for live singing events at sports stadia.
