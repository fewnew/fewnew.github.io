YOU'LL NEVER WALK ALONE

Follow these step by step instructions to take part in this project.

What you'll need: Laptop/iPad, earphones, internet & your voice
Estimated time: 3 minutes

1. Put your earphones in!

2. Take a quick listen to the song as sung by 100's of UK Football Sports Fans (Video 1)

3. Click “allow” to enable recording on video. You should see yourself now (Video 2)

4. Go back to the beginning of Video 1 and click the red record button on Video 2. You should see the timer start. This means you're recording and you can start singing anytime. Don't worry if it takes a moment to start singing. You have 5 minutes if you need it.

5. You can try again if you like and save when you're done. That's it!
*Remember: it doesn't need to be perfect! Your voice/face will be gathered with many others! (Just remember to use your earphones so that we just record your voice!)

Thank you!
