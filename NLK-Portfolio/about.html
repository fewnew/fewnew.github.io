Hi there, I'm Leily Khatibi. I design, produce and create human-centered experiences.

I am passionate about creating aesthetically pleasing, interactive experiences, and telling stories using cutting-edge technology. I am eager to develop quality, visual designs with a human centric concentration, in various scales, by working with multidisciplinary teams.

In addition to achieving practical skills in studying Architecture for my undergraduate degree, I acquired an understanding of human nature and our relationship with physical spaces. I am currently expanding my digital toolset by pursing a Master of Fine Arts degree in Digital Media Art. My area of focus is building hybrid (hardware+software) artifacts using new media technologies like physical computing, mixed reality, and modern web applications. I blend art, design, architecture, and technology to create memorable and inspiring experiences for people.
